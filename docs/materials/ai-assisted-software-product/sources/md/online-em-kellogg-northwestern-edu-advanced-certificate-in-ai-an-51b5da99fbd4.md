## Program Overview: Advanced Certificate in AI and Product Strategy 
The four-month **Advanced Certificate in AI and Product Strategy** offers a unique opportunity to explore the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and product innovation through two expert-led programs from Kellogg Executive Education — AI Strategies for Business Transformation: Generative and Agentic Intelligence and AI-Driven Product Strategy.  
  
The product leadership landscape is evolving as AI redefines how organizations innovate and scale. The AI Strategies for Business Transformation: Generative and Agentic Intelligence program empowers forward-thinking business leaders to leverage AI technologies and generative AI (gen AI) for strategic impact across key areas, including customer experience, talent productivity, new product development and personalized marketing. The AI-Driven Product Strategy program empowers professionals to lead the entire product life cycle with an AI-forward curriculum, from vision and discovery to go-to-market (GTM) strategy and growth.  
  
Together, these programs provide a comprehensive learning experience that equips participants with actionable insights and practical skills to lead AI integration, product development and innovation with strategic clarity and AI-driven confidence.

## Who Is the Advanced Certificate in AI and Product Strategy For? 
The Advanced Certificate in AI and Product Strategy is tailored for professionals looking to lead with confidence in AI-driven markets. Whether optimizing product portfolios, driving innovation or scaling operations, this program equips participants with the tools and insights needed to excel in a rapidly evolving landscape.
**Chief product officers, product leaders and mid-level to senior product managers** seeking to evolve into AI-first strategists, optimize workflows, accelerate decision making and align cross-functional teams to innovate, scale and meet growing market demands.
**Strategic decision-makers and functional heads** across marketing, sales, operations and IT aiming to integrate AI into product strategies and business processes, foster a culture of innovation, drive strong AI adoption and build sustainable business models.
**Founders and business leaders** aiming to translate AI opportunities into scalable products, improve operational efficiency and align product strategy with organizational goals while ensuring growth, strong AI adoption and relevance.
## What You Will Learn
The **AI** **Strategies for Business Transformation** program will enable you to: 
  * Assess the impact of AI, gen AI and agentic AI on business growth, productivity and innovation in diverse organizational contexts 
  * Apply frameworks to assess AI readiness, including the AI Canvas 2.0, AI Radar 2.0 and AI Capability Maturity Model (CMM)
  * Identify high-value AI use cases across customer experience, operations and support functions — tailored to industry needs
  * Recognize the ethical, governance and societal implications of AI, ensuring responsible implementation
  * Design an AI transformation plan and a strategic transformation roadmap for an organization


The **AI-Driven Product Strategy** program will enable you to: 
  * Design a strategic product vision and roadmap that aligns with corporate goals and motivates organizational alignment
  * Analyze product opportunities using frameworks and AI-supported insights to inform strategic decisions
  * ​Build and deliver monetization and GTM strategies that drive product-led growth (PLG)
  * Apply proven practices in product development, pricing and growth to deliver long-term business value
  * Evaluate communication and influence strategies to strengthen stakeholder alignment and decision making

## Capstone Projects
In the **AI Strategies for Business Transformation: Generative and Agentic Intelligence** program, you will gain practical expertise in building AI capabilities, ensuring ethical governance and fostering cross-functional collaboration to drive success. For this project, you will assume the role of a manager responsible for an AI project and craft a comprehensive “Memo to the CEO” that: 
  * Synthesizes the insights from user interviews to create a list of Jobs-To-Be-Done (JTBD) and pain points, organized by department or function 
  * Includes a detailed data governance framework for the client that will form the foundation for AI initiatives 
  * Presents a business case for each high-value AI use case using the AI Canvas 2.0
