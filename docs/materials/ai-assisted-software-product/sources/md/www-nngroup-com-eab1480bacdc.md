## Why NN/g
  * **We’re a team of experts.** We’ve spent more than 25 years working in research and design — serving a wide variety of industries and clients, from small startups to Fortune 500 companies.
  * **We’re trusted for our research-backed guidance.** We publish actionable research findings to establish industry best practices for UX professionals to use in their work.
  * **We give practical advice.** Our customers choose to come back to us time after time because we give them recommendations that work. No gimmicks, trends, or buzzwords here.
